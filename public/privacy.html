Privacy Policy - Vysion Horeca

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Vysion Horeca, part of Vysion Group International, values the protection of your personal data. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why we do this, and how we handle it.

2. What data do we collect?

  • Name and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Business information (company name, VAT number, address)
  • Payment details
  • Usage data of our software
  • Communication you have with us

3. Why do we use your data?

  • To provide our services and software
  • To process payments
  • Customer service and support
  • To improve our products
  • Communication about updates and new features
  • Compliance with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data very seriously. Our systems are secured with SSL/TLS encryption, encrypted storage, access control, regular security audits, and daily backups.

5. Sharing of Data

We only share your data with third parties when necessary for our services (such as payment processors) or when legally required. We never sell your data to third parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or transfer your data. You can also object to processing.
